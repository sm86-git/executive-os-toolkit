# Board-ready narrative outline

## 1) The story (one paragraph)
What happened and why it matters.

## 2) Scorecard
5-8 metrics with targets and trends.

## 3) Key drivers
Top 3 drivers of results, with evidence.

## 4) Risks and mitigations
Only material risks with clear ownership.

## 5) Decisions requested
What you need from the board or leadership.

## 6) Next moves
3-5 priorities for the next period.

Rule:
If a slide needs more than one sentence to explain, rewrite the slide.
