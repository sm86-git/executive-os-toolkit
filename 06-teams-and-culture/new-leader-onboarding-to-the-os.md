# New leader onboarding to the operating system

## Day 1-7
- Share the cadence map and artifacts
- Review health definitions and decision log norms

## Day 8-30
- Join weekly exec staff and monthly review
- Take ownership of one artifact (status, RAID, metrics)

## Day 31-60
- Propose one improvement to reduce noise or increase speed
- Confirm responsibilities in DACI/RAPID for key decisions
