# Annual planning calendar (example)

## Weeks 1-2: Set direction
- Exec goals and constraints
- KPI definitions and targets

## Weeks 3-5: Build plans
- Functional plans and resource needs
- Cross-functional dependency review

## Weeks 6-7: Tradeoff workshops
- Resolve conflicts
- Confirm "must win" bets

## Weeks 8-9: Finalize
- Publish plan narrative and KPI targets
- Lock operating cadence for the year
