# Resource tradeoff workshop

## Purpose
Force explicit tradeoffs across initiatives and capacity.

## Pre-work
- List top initiatives with effort and impact
- Current capacity by team
- Non-negotiables (compliance, reliability, commitments)

## Agenda (90 minutes)
1. Confirm constraints (10 min)
2. Review initiative slate (20 min)
3. Rank by impact and urgency (20 min)
4. Identify conflicts and dependencies (20 min)
5. Make tradeoffs and record decisions (15 min)
6. Close: comms plan (5 min)

## Outputs
- Updated priority order
- Deferred items and rationale
- Resource shifts captured
