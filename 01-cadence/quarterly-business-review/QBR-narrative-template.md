# QBR narrative template (2 pages)

## 1) Context
What was the plan and what assumptions were we making?

## 2) Results
What happened relative to plan? What did we learn?

## 3) Drivers
What were the top 3 drivers of outcomes (good and bad)?

## 4) Risks and constraints
What are we worried about and why now?

## 5) Decisions needed
List decisions, recommended path, and impact.

## 6) Next quarter priorities
3-5 priorities with success measures.
