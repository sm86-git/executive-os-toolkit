# QBR deck outline (10-12 slides)

1. Narrative: the quarter in one slide
2. KPI scorecard (targets vs actuals)
3. What changed since last quarter
4. Customer and market signals
5. Product and delivery progress (milestones)
6. Quality and reliability
7. GTM and pipeline health (if relevant)
8. Top risks and how we are mitigating
9. Decisions and tradeoffs requested
10. Next quarter priorities (3-5)
11. Resource view (optional)
12. Appendix (deep dives)
