# MOR metrics pack outline

Keep it consistent month to month.

## 1) Executive summary (1 page)
- What improved
- What declined
- What needs a decision

## 2) Revenue and growth
- ARR, NARR, bookings (as applicable)
- Pipeline coverage (if relevant)
- Renewal and expansion signals

## 3) Customer and product health
- Adoption and usage
- Support volume and themes
- Quality and reliability

## 4) Delivery and execution
- Milestone delivery
- Cycle time and throughput
- Top blockers

## 5) People and capacity
- Headcount and hiring plan
- Attrition and critical roles
- Burnout indicators and hotspots
