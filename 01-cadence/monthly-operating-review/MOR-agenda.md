# Monthly Operating Review (MOR) agenda

## Purpose
Review business health, validate priorities, and resolve cross-functional tradeoffs.

## Inputs (pre-read)
- Metrics pack
- Portfolio health and milestones
- Top risks and dependencies
- Key customer, product, or operational signals

## Agenda (60-90 minutes)
1. Business health and trends (20-30 min)
2. Portfolio and delivery health (20-30 min)
3. Risks, dependencies, and resourcing (15-20 min)
4. Decisions and tradeoffs (10-20 min)
5. Close: actions and comms (5 min)

## Outputs
- Decisions and tradeoffs captured
- Updated priorities and resource shifts (if any)
- Action list and owners
