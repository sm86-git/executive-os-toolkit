# Weekly Exec Staff agenda template

## Purpose
Align the exec team on what changed, make needed decisions, and remove blockers.

## Pre-read (sent 24 hours prior)
- Weekly exec summary (1 page)
- Top risks and dependencies
- Decisions needed this week

## Agenda (45-60 minutes)
1. Opening (2 min)
   - Confirm outcomes for the meeting: decisions, escalations, actions

2. What changed (10 min)
   - Wins since last week
   - Material changes to priorities, metrics, or timelines

3. Decisions needed (15-25 min)
   - Each decision: 3 minutes context, 5 minutes discussion, 2 minutes call
   - Capture decision, owner, and rationale in the Decision Log

4. Top risks and dependencies (10-15 min)
   - Focus only on items that need exec help this week
   - Confirm owners and next steps

5. Comms and alignment (5 min)
   - What will we tell the org?
   - Who needs a heads up?

6. Close (3 min)
   - Review actions and due dates
   - Confirm next meeting pre-read owner

## Output
- Updated decision log
- Action list (owner, due date)
- Escalations and comms notes
