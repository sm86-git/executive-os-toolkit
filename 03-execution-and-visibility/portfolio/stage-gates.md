# Stage gates

## 1) Idea
Exit criteria:
- Clear problem statement
- Rough success measures
- Owner identified

## 2) Discovery
Exit criteria:
- Options explored
- Risks and dependencies identified
- Rough plan and milestones

## 3) Delivery
Exit criteria:
- Milestones tracked
- RAID maintained
- Status is consistent

## 4) Launch
Exit criteria:
- Launch checklist complete
- Comms plan executed
- Post-launch metrics defined

## 5) Post-launch
Exit criteria:
- Results reviewed
- Learnings captured
- Next investments decided
