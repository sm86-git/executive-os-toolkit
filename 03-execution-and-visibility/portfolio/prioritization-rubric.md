# Prioritization rubric (example)

Score each 1-5.

## Value
- Customer impact
- Revenue or cost impact
- Risk reduction

## Urgency
- External deadline
- Strategic sequencing
- Cost of delay

## Effort
- Engineering effort
- Cross-functional complexity
- Operational burden

## Risk
- Technical risk
- Delivery risk
- Reputational / compliance risk

Tip:
Use the rubric to drive conversation, not to pretend it is math.
