# Health definitions

## Green
- On track to hit commitments
- No material risks without mitigation
- Dependencies are under control

## Yellow
- At risk without leadership attention or a tradeoff
- One or more dependencies is uncertain
- Scope, timeline, or resourcing needs adjustment

## Red
- Unlikely to hit the commitment without major change
- Critical dependency is blocked or failing
- Immediate decision or escalation required

## Confidence (probability of hitting the target)
- High: 80-100%
- Medium: 50-79%
- Low: below 50%
