# Contributing

Thanks for your interest in improving this toolkit.

## What to contribute
- New templates that are broadly applicable and vendor-neutral
- Improvements to clarity, structure, or examples
- Fixes to typos and broken links

## Guidelines
- Keep language simple and actionable
- Avoid proprietary references, customer details, or internal processes
- Prefer checklists and definitions over long prose
- Include at least one filled example for any new template

## Pull request checklist
- [ ] Template has a clear purpose statement at the top
- [ ] Includes "Inputs" and "Outputs"
- [ ] Uses consistent terminology with existing artifacts
- [ ] No sensitive or identifying information
