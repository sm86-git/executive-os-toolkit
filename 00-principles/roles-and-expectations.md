# Roles and expectations

## Executive sponsor
- Sets priorities and approves tradeoffs
- Holds the team accountable to decisions and follow-through

## Chief of Staff / PMO lead
- Designs and runs the cadence
- Owns artifact quality, hygiene, and visibility
- Facilitates decisions and escalations

## Functional leaders
- Provide inputs on progress, risks, and dependencies
- Come prepared with options and recommendations

## Program / initiative leads
- Maintain plans, milestones, and RAID
- Escalate early with clear asks

## Team norms
- Pre-reads are the default
- Meetings end with written outputs
- If it is not in the log, it did not happen
